### Minesweeper

**Objective:**   
Some tiles have bombs underneath. Identify the safe tiles and click only on them to win the game.

**Rules:**  
Start with a random tile (and hope you’re not extremely unlucky). Clicking on a tile may or may not reveal some of the nearby tiles. Any number written on a tile denotes the precise number of bombs adjacent to that tile (for example, if a tile in the middle of the field says 2, then exactly two of the 8 adjacent tiles will have a bomb. Click and reveal all the safe tiles.
