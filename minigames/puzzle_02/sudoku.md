### DECRYPTION TACTICS: 6x6 Sudoku Matrix
**Objective:** Restore the corrupted 6x6 data transmission by filling in the missing integers.

**Cryptographic Constraints:**
To successfully decrypt the grid, you must satisfy three absolute logical conditions:
1. Every **row** must contain the numbers 1 through 6 exactly once.
2. Every **column** must contain the numbers 1 through 6 exactly once.
3. Every **$2 \times 3$ subgrid** (highlighted by the thicker neon borders) must contain the numbers 1 through 6 exactly once.

**Controls:**
Click on any empty cell and type a number from 1 to 6. If your logic holds, the matrix will automatically stabilize and decrypt once the final correct digit is entered.
