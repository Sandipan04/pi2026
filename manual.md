### Mission Guide

Among millions of ways for calculating the value of $\\pi$, one of the fastest experimental approximations is by taking the ratio of the number of coprime points on a lattice to the whole lattice. **PROJECT WW 3.14159** is a collaborative, coordinate mapping simulation, where the players bombard a lattice grid to score individual points and compete in a leaderboard. To buy better nukes, there are interesting cryptic minigames which earn you **supply points** which can be redeemed in the shop.

While you enjoy bombarding the lattice grid, your contributions improve the current approximation of the value of $\\pi$ on the website, visible on the left panel. The more you nuke, the higher you climb on the leaderboard, while you can parallely see how you helped in the approximation.

**THE ENEMY BASE**

* **Mission Objective:** Your goal is to find "Enemy Bases" hidden exclusively at coprime coordinate intersections, and drop powerful bombs (with varying areas of coverage).  
* **Controls:**  
  * *Desktop:* Left-Click to fire. Click & Drag to pan the camera. Scroll to zoom.  
  * *Mobile:* Tap to fire. 2-Finger drag to pan. Pinch to zoom.

**MUNITIONS AND SHOP**

* **Supply Points:** The currency of the command center. You earn Supply Points by successfully decrypting the puzzles in the **Arcade**.  
* **Munitions Hub:** A standard Mk-1 payload only bombs a single coordinate. Spend your Supply Points in the Munitions Hub to obtain heavy cluster bombs and nukes, allowing you to sweep massive areas of the grid with a single fire.
