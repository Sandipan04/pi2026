### 🌐 GLOBAL COMMAND MANUAL

**PROJECT WW 3.14159** is a collaborative, cryptographic coordinate mapping simulation. You and other commanders are tasked with uncovering hidden enemy infrastructure on a massive global grid to approximate the tactical value of Pi.

**THE TACTICAL RADAR**
* **The Grid:** A sprawling battlefield. Your objective is to find "Radar Bases" hidden exclusively at coprime coordinate intersections.
* **Controls:** * *Desktop:* Left-Click to fire. Click & Drag to pan the camera. Scroll to zoom.
  * *Mobile:* Tap to fire. 2-Finger drag to pan. Pinch to zoom.
* **The War Effort:** Every base found contributes to the global algorithm tier, permanently pushing the simulation closer to absolute mathematical precision.

**THE ECONOMY & MUNITIONS**
* **Supply Points:** The currency of the command center. You earn Supply Points by successfully decrypting intercepted signals in the **Crypto Arcade**.
* **Munitions Hub:** A standard Mk-1 payload only reveals a single coordinate. Spend your Supply Points in the Munitions Hub to requisition heavy-yield cluster bombs and devastators, allowing you to sweep massive areas of the grid with a single strike.
